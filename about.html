About Us

Our story

Founded by bakers who love good bread, Baker's Dozen began as a small neighbourhood bakery and grew from a passion for traditional baking techniques and locally sourced ingredients. We bake each day in small batches so every loaf and pastry gets the attention it deserves.

Our mission

To bring warm, honest baking to our community: simple, seasonal recipes made with care. We prioritise quality, sustainability, and a friendly customer experience.

  • Handcrafted daily
  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • No artificial preservatives
  • Community-focused

Local Ingredients

We source from local farms where possible, supporting growers and keeping flavours fresh.

Made with Care

Our team treats every bake with attention to detail — no shortcuts, just good baking.

Tradition & Taste

Combining traditional techniques with modern flavors to create memorable bread and patisseries.
